**Gridle** is a premium, high-performance Formula 1 Wordle clone built for motorsport fans. Test your F1 knowledge by guessing the mystery driver based on their team, age, nationality, and career stats.

Designed with a sleek dark mode, glassmorphic UI elements, dynamic flag/logo icons, persistent local statistics, and a thrilling real-time multiplayer backend. 

## ✨ Game Modes

- 🗓️ **Daily F1 Wordle Challenge**: One mystery driver per day, synced globally. Can you guess who it is in 6 tries?
- ♾️ **Infinite Practice Mode**: Keep the fun going as much as you want with a randomized selection of drivers.
- ⚔️ **Real-Time 1v1 Multiplayer**: Create a room, share the 5-letter code, and race a friend live! See their guesses in real-time on a synchronized mini-board. First to guess the driver wins! 

## 🌟 Features

- 📊 **Persistent Statistics**: Tracks your played games, win percentage, current streak, max streak, and your exact guess distribution locally.
- 💡 **Interactive Hint System**: Unlock dynamic driver hints as you progress (available in Daily and Infinite modes, disabled in 1v1 for fair play).
- 🎨 **Premium UI/UX**: High-contrast, glassmorphic UI matching F1's signature red accent aesthetics with micro-animations and confetti celebrations.

## 🎮 How to Play

### Single Player (Daily & Infinite)
1. Type and select a driver from the F1 database.
2. Submit your guess to reveal how close your driver's stats are to the target:
   - **Driver**: Correct name indicator.
   - **Nationality**: Green for correct country.
   - **Team**: Green for correct team.
   - **Age**: Green for correct age, or an arrow indicating if the target driver is older (🔼) or younger (🔽).
   - **Wins**: Green for correct win count, or an arrow indicating if the target driver has more (🔼) or fewer (🔽) career wins.
3. Use hints if you get stuck (unlocked after 3 guesses).

### 1v1 Multiplayer Race
1. Switch to **1v1** mode and click **Create Room**.
2. Share the generated 5-letter code with your opponent.
3. Once they join, the 3-2-1 countdown begins!
4. You both get the **same mystery driver**. You can see your opponent's grid colors (but not who they guessed) in real-time.
5. **First to guess the correct driver wins.** If both players use all 6 guesses without finding the driver, it's a draw!
6. Click **Rematch** to play again with a new driver in the same room.
